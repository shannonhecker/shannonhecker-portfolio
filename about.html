about me

my approach.

I anchor design in outcomes:
usable today, scalable tomorrow,
accessible always.

Every project starts with user and business constraints. I validate direction through prototyping, research, and tight feedback loops with product and engineering.

Design systems and modern tooling, including AI where it earns its place, speed delivery without trading off clarity. Whether untangling a dense dashboard or defining patterns across a platform, the goal is inclusive, maintainable UX that teams can ship and iterate.
