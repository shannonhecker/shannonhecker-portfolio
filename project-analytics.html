Fusion Analytics Dashboard

A flagship internal product to modernize how trading and ops teams access high-volume data, with intuitive data selection and interactive visualizations built on Fusion Data Cubes.

about.

Project Overview

I worked on Fusion's new performance analytics features, which provide detailed insights into returns, attribution, and risk across asset classes. We partnered with FactSet to integrate their analytics engine, allowing asset managers to focus on alpha generation while using Fusion's clean, unified interface for data and board-ready reporting.

The challenge was creating intuitive, board-quality dashboards that translate complex analytics into clear, actionable insights. It had to handle advanced filtering, deep slicing of data, and remain user-friendly under pressure, all while modernizing the legacy RaaS UI into a responsive web-based flex layout.

My Role

As Product Designer and UI Lead, I owned the visual direction and interaction design across the analytics platform, working closely with a UX designer and product owner embedded in cross-functional teams. I defined the component structure and accessibility layers in Figma with Salt Design System, translating trading desk needs into modular, reusable patterns that ensured accessible, cohesive experiences across every module.

Design Process

I collaborated with the product owner to gather requirements and align on goals, then worked closely with UX designers to iterate on mockups. We reviewed designs with the product and development teams, incorporating feedback and ensuring accessibility with an ADA specialist.

Once finalized, I partnered with developers to ensure pixel-perfect implementation, maintaining daily standups to keep the team aligned and on track. The dashboard was designed to handle large datasets, both real-time and historical, with filtering, toggles, aggregation views, and comparison tools. I kept the interface modular to adapt to varying data cube complexity and user role permissions.
